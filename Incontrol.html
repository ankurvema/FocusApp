Four Pillars of InControl

📅

Plan

Turn today's to-dos into clear time blocks. Drag tasks into 25-120 minute sessions with automatic break suggestions. No more decision paralysis.

⏱️

Focus

Use a clean, full-screen timer with optional calming sounds like rain, nature, or white noise. Start with one tap and stay in the zone.

🎖️

Motivate

Earn gentle badges and celebrate small wins. Build streaks and see your progress with positive reinforcement that actually feels good.

💡

Reflect

See simple usage insights to understand what works and make better choices tomorrow. No judgment, just helpful data.

A Day with InControl

1

Morning (2 minutes)

Pick 3-5 tasks and drag them into a simple plan for your day. Clear, visual, and stress-free planning.

2

During Work

Start a 50-minute focus session with rain sounds. Get a gentle reminder when it's time for a short break.

3

After Lunch

Use the "Starter" nudge to get going again. Earn a streak badge for completing day 3 of consistent focus.

4

Evening

Check the insights screen to see what worked and what didn't, then plan tomorrow's first block with confidence.

What Makes InControl Different

✨ ADHD-Friendly by Design

Clear steps, low friction, supportive language, and dyslexia-friendly font options. Every feature is designed with ADHD in mind.

⚡ Action-First

Helps you decide and start now—not just another long to-do list that makes you feel overwhelmed.

🎯 Positive Motivation

Small wins, streaks, and gentle celebrations. No guilt, no pressure, just encouragement to keep going.

🔒 Privacy-Respecting

Your personal usage information stays on your device unless you choose otherwise. No judgmental language or dark patterns.

🌈 Hyperfocus Recovery

Unique feature that suggests quick light tasks to help you reset when you've gone too long—fold clothes, tidy desk, short walk.

🎨 Comfortable Experience

Large tap areas, light/dark themes, clear text, and accessibility options. Designed for real human brains.
