1) Quick summary

  • We collect information you provide (like account and content), and information generated automatically (like device data and limited usage analytics).
  • We use data to operate the App, provide features (e.g., backup, sync, notifications), secure our Services, comply with law, and improve performance.
  • We share data with service providers and, if applicable, for analytics/attribution. We do not sell your personal information.
  • You can access, update, export, or delete your data by contacting us at ankuriosdev@gmail.com or using in-app settings (if available).

2) What we collect

The categories of information we collect depend on how you use the Services:

  • Account data: name, email, username, avatar, and authentication identifiers.
  • Subscription & payments: product, status, and receipts (processed by Apple/Google; we do not store full card data).
  • App content: items you create or upload in the App (e.g., tasks, notes, timers, routines).
  • Device & usage: app version, device model, OS version, time zone, language, crash logs, and basic interactions (e.g., session counts, screens visited).
  • Diagnostics: performance metrics and crash reports to fix bugs and improve stability.
  • Support: information you send us (e.g., emails, attachments, feedback).
  • Optional integrations (only if you connect them): calendar, reminders, or third-party sign-in—subject to their permissions and policies.

Examples of common processors (adjust to your setup): Apple/Google for purchases, Firebase Analytics, RevenueCat for subscriptions, Sentry for crash reporting.

  • Personalize content (e.g., saved preferences, localization, reminders).
  • Monitor performance, debug, and prevent fraud, spam, or abuse.
  • Communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms.

Legal bases (EEA/UK only): performance of a contract, legitimate interests (e.g., security, product improvement), compliance with law, and consent where required (e.g., optional analytics or marketing).

4) How we share information

We do not sell personal information. We do not share for targeted advertising unless explicitly stated and consented (if required in your region).
